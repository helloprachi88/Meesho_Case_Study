# Meesho Logistics Collaboration Case Study

## Project Overview

This project is a Business Analysis case study focused on analyzing operational challenges in the logistics ecosystem of Meesho.

The objective of this project was to study how poor collaboration between sellers, warehouse teams, logistics partners, and customer support impacts delivery performance and customer experience, and propose business solutions to improve operational efficiency.

---

## Business Problem

Meesho’s logistics operations involve coordination between multiple stakeholders.

Current operational challenges identified:

* Sellers dispatching orders late
* Inventory mismatch between sellers and warehouse teams
* Delayed shipment pickup by logistics partners
* Lack of real-time shipment visibility
* Communication gaps between operational teams

Because of these issues, customers experience delivery delays and order cancellations.

---

## Project Objective

The main objective of this project was to:

* Understand the logistics workflow at Meesho
* Identify operational bottlenecks affecting delivery performance
* Analyze the impact of poor stakeholder collaboration
* Evaluate important business KPIs
* Recommend process improvements to improve operational efficiency

---

## Business Analysis Approach

The project was completed using the following approach:

### Step 1 — Problem Identification

Studied the logistics workflow and identified coordination issues between operational teams.

### Step 2 — Stakeholder Analysis

Mapped responsibilities of:

* Sellers
* Warehouse Team
* Logistics Partners
* Customer Support Team
* Operations Team

### Step 3 — KPI Analysis

Evaluated business performance using operational metrics such as:

* Average Delivery Time
* Order Cancellation Rate
* Customer Complaint Rate
* Seller SLA Compliance
* On-Time Delivery Rate

### Step 4 — Root Cause Analysis

Identified major causes of inefficiency:

* Poor seller and warehouse communication
* Inventory management issues
* Delayed logistics partner coordination
* Lack of centralized shipment tracking

### Step 5 — Solution Recommendation

Proposed operational improvements to improve collaboration and delivery performance.

---

## Proposed Solutions

Recommended solutions:

* Centralized Logistics Dashboard
* Automated Inventory Synchronization
* SLA Monitoring System
* Integrated Logistics Collaboration Platform
* Real-Time Shipment Visibility

---

## Expected Business Impact

Expected improvements after implementation:

* Delivery Time reduced by 22%
* Order Cancellation reduced by 18%
* Customer Complaints reduced by 25%
* Seller SLA Compliance improved to 90%
* Customer Satisfaction improved significantly
